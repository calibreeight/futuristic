Frequently Asked Questions

Do we outsource our work?

No outsourcing, black hat tactics, or cutting corners. All of the work that is conducted on our client’s campaigns is conducted in-house by our own team of digital marketing experts right here in Melbourne, Australia. You can rest assured that you are dealing with some of the country’s brightest and most talented marketing professionals. Most importantly – we would never risk our client’s businesses and their brands with somebody that isn’t accountable.

Do we guarantee first page results?                                 

It is simply impossible for us (or any agency) to provide guarantees of first page rankings. Any agency that claims otherwise will usually be doing so for low search volume easy to rank keywords. However, our team of SEO experts have been successfully ranking websites on the first page of Google in competitive markets for years.

How much do our services cost?

Our pricing structure is customised to the specific requirements of each client and their particular business in their industry. No two businesses are the same, and after one of our SEO consultants gets to know your digital marketing needs better, we will be able to provide you with a competitive quote.

Will I be locked into a long term contract?

We don't lock clients into long term contracts. All our clients are put on a month-to-month arrangement. This puts the pressure back on us to perform, because we understand that without results, eventually, clients will leave us. That forces our whole team to focus around producing results for clients.
